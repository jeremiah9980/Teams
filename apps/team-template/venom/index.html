The Expectation

Team Standards

Our standards are set high both on and off the field. These expectations apply to every player and family in our organization, regardless of age group.

Off The Field

1

Schedules are shared as early as possible so families can flag conflicts ahead of time.

2

We ask for at least a one-week notice on missed practices or games — unless illness, injury, or family emergency. It lets us plan reps and roster properly.

3

We fully support multi-sport athletes. But missed time or repeated tardiness without communication can impact opportunities and playing time.

On The Field

✦

Hustle on and off the field.

✦

Maintain a positive attitude.

✦

Be a supportive teammate.

✦

Give maximum focus, attitude, and effort.

If standards aren't being met, there's one conversation with player and parent as a final warning. Every player is held to the same standard regardless of skill level.

Our Why

Why The Standard

Success at the highest levels of this game isn't talent alone — it's standards, accountability, discipline, attitude, effort, and respect. That's the foundation we're building here.

These aren't just 12U or 14U expectations. They apply at every level of the game — from youth softball all the way to college and professional athletics.

As the organization grows, more families are reaching out because of the culture and the standard. We owe it to every committed player to protect that.
